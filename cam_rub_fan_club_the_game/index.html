Welcome to Cam Rub Fan club the game (Critter Edition)

Instructions

Overview

Welcome to Cam Rub Fan Club the game! Everyone here is friends but they still want to win. To win the game you need to get the most points, and points are gained by collecting gems. The real winner is the one that has the most fun.

Characters

Cam Rub, Trent, Stew and Winnie, are the characters of the game. It's one boy and one girl to a team. Boys are grey, and girls are white. Teams get color tokens with velcro on them. It does not matter who plays with who, except to have one boy and girl per team because they move differently. At the beginning of the game boys and girls placed as shown below. Boy are kitty-corner to boys, and girls are kitty corner to girls. Girls move diagonally like bishops in chess, and boys move horizontally and vertically. Both move one square per turn. The way characters move is the most exciting part of the game (it's kind of like checkers) see that section for the details.

Setting up the game

First choose a team, either orange or green. Then choose a boy and a girl character. Put both on the velcro of the team tokens. Place each character as show on the character map. The next step is to place the first gem. Press space bar on the display to generate an (almost) random position on the board. This location is where the gem is placed. Rock paper scissors, or an agreed upon game of skill determines which player plays first.

Playing a turn

On each turn a player gets to choose one piece to move, if they choose a girl, it follows the girls movement, if they choose a boy it follows the boys movement. If a player lands on the current gem they collect it and place it in their scoring area for the points that it is worth. Once the gem is collected a new gem location is generated by running the generator. After this, it's the next players turn.

Scoring points

Each reigon of the map has different points. Points increase from the inside of the board outward. The scoring is Blue:1, Green:2, Red: 3. You will need to be clever when deciding if you want to go for points near the center, if they are worth your time or not.

Movement

The winner of the game will be decided by how clever their movements are, this is the most technically challenging part of the game. By default boys move horizontally or vertically one position and girls move diagonally one position.

You can use other plays to boost you move! If your move would have you land on another character you can use their movement direction. In addition to your own (kind of like checkers). In the examples below a boy uses a girls movement, and the other way around.

If you jump on a member of your own team you get to steal a single move just like you see above. But if you jump on the member of another team you can take either one, or two of their moves. Its up to you to decide whats best

Just like checkers you can chain multiple jumps together. Feel free to bounce around until your hearts content and your strategy is exacted.

Figuring out the best way to chain together moves, and how to defend against them is the challenge of the game.
