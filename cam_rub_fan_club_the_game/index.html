Welcome to Cam Rub Fan club the game (Critter Edition)

Instructions

Overview

Welcome to Cam Rub Fan Club the game. Everyone here is friends but they still want to win! To win the game collect tokens and get the most points. Token locations are generated by the "Next Token" generator on this page. This game is for two players, but feel free to play with more.

Characters

Cam Rub, Trent, Stew and Winnie, are the protagonists. They are paired into teams of two. One boy and one Girl each. Boys and girls think differently and in this game they move differently too.

In General boys are lighter and girls are darker colors (but it's really their physiques that separate them) Teams get color tokens with velcro on them. It does not matter what the teams are, except to have one boy and girl per team.

Setting up the game

First choose a team, either orange or green. Then choose a boy and a girl for your roster. Put both on the velcro of the team tokens. Place each character on their starting position. Next generate the first token location by pressing "r" or clicking the Next Token button. Place the token on it's color and number. The player to go first is decided with rock, paper, scisors, or another agreed upon game. Examples: Truth or Dare, Twister, Spin the bottle, ect..

Playing a turn

Players alternate turns. On your turn you must move one character. Characters follow the movement rules detailed below (it's a bit like chess, and a bit like checkers). If a player lands on the current gem they collect it and then generate a new token using the generator.

Scoring points

Tokens on different colored regions of the map are with different points.

Blue: 1
Green: 2
Red: 3

Lower value colors are generated more often. This means the center of the board is most likely to generate a token. Be strategic about which tokens you collect.

Movement

Girls move diagonally, and Boys move vertically and horizontally. Characters move a single unit by default, but they can hopscotch off another character if they land on them. Hopscotching off a team member lets you make one additional move using the movement direction of the character you landed on. If a girl lands on a boy she can move one square vertically, or one horizontally and vice versa. If you land on an opponent you can choose to move either one or two square using the same rules as above. Below are examples of player movements.

Just like checkers you can chain multiple jumps together. Feel free to bounce around until your hearts content and your strategy is exacted.

Figuring out the best way to chain together moves, and how to defend against them is the challenge of the game.
